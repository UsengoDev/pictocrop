About Pictocrop

Fast, free, and privacy-focused image cropping tool.

Our Mission

Pictocrop’s mission is to give users a simple, client-side tool to crop images without compromising privacy or speed.

How It Works

All cropping happens in your browser. Images never leave your device. Crop, resize, and adjust your images instantly and securely.

Why Choose Pictocrop

  • No uploads — your privacy is protected
  • Fast client-side processing
  • Supports multiple formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
  • Drag, resize, and crop with ease
